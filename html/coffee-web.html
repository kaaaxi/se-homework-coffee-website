Flona Coffee Bean

$79.99

This is a coffee full of love, has been known by three names. Starbucks first created this coffee bean for a Seattle restaurant in 1975, when it was named Jack Integrated Coffee Bean. Due to its popularity, Starbucks began hand-blending the coffee beans for customers in its stores, and decided to name it 80/20 Blend coffee based on the blending ratio. The enduring love and admiration of Starbucks customers for this coffee eventually led to its inclusion in the official Starbucks product line, named Flona, in honor of the city's many inspirations. No matter which name, it conveys the romantic story of this coffee.
